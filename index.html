What is Vute?


The site is intended to provide rooted discussions with rich formatting.
Users are able to paste in formatted responses with images from the
internet, links, etc. into their comment space, and that formatting is
preserved (to some extent) in the rendered version of the comment.

Annotation


Annotations are made my highlighting part of a root document.
Root content is any document found in the category “baseContent”
Each annotation provides a space for the user who selected the section
to mention briefly why they noted it.

When making annotations an alert will appear on your screen.
The space for your comment is filled with a … placeholder until you
provide some content.

Don’t forget to save your comments!
